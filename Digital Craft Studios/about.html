About Us

It all started with Our Founder's Idea

At DigitalCraft Studios, to make art part of our everyday life, we are always on the lookout to find newer ways of experiencing Indian art and make that experience delightful.

We started Our Studio in 2021

Although atfirst we started as an online gallery but now you’ll find us collaborating with physical galleries, consultants, and our on-ground partners to create a distinct experience for novices and veterans alike.

Our Idea of Change

By bringing art & artists online we believe we’re helping make art more accessible. We seek to transform every person's experience with art into one of inclusion, belonging, and personal expression through our contemporary art curation and engagement.

Our Future plan with You All

We invite you to join us in our creative movement as we passionately build a space for every stakeholder in the art world - artists, art collectors, curators, framing partners and others.
